Task 1: Pseudocode an Everyday Task

How to Make Guacamole

1. Cut 3 avocados in half
2. Remove the seeds from the avocados
3. Scoop out avocados into large mixing bowl
4. Mash avocados until you get desired guac consistency
5. Cut onion in half
6. Dice half of onion
7. Add to medium mixing bowl
8. Cut jalapeño in half
9. Remove seeds from jalapeño
10. Dice jalapeño
11. Add to the same medium mixing bowl
12. Cut of ends of two tomatoes
13. Dice up the two tomatoes
14. Add to the same medium mixing bowl
15. Remove stems from cilantro
16. Chop 1/4 cup cilantro
17. Add to the same medium mixing bowl
18. Cut lime in half
19. Squeeze lime over mixing bowl ingredients
20. Sprinkle salt into mixing bowl
21. Mix ingredients in the medium mixing bowl
22. Add medium mixing bowl ingredients into large mixing bowl with avocado
23. Stir all ingredients until evenly mixed
24. Add more ingredients for desired taste

Task 2: Pseudocode a Computer Task

How to Change a Mac Desktop Folder

1. Think of a subject you would want as an icon to your folders
2. Create your own png in an Adobe software app such as Photoshop or a website such as Canva
3. If you are unable to do the previous step, look up "(your subject) png" in your preferred search browser
4. Check that your file is a png with no background
5. Drag the png you would like to change it to to your desktop and choose Edit > Copy
6. Click the designated folder on your desktop you would like to change from Apple's given blue folder icon to the icon of your choice
7. Right click the folder and select File > Get Info
8. Once on the file preview, click the small icon in the top left corner and choose Edit > Paste
9. Repeat steps if you would like to do it more than once
