How to Make Guacamole

Cut 3 avocados in half Remove the seeds from the avocados Scoop out avocados into large mixing bowl Mash avocados until you get desired guac consistency Cut onion in half Dice half of onion Add to medium mixing bowl Cut jalapeño in half Remove seeds from jalapeño Dice jalapeno Add to the same medium mixing bowl Cut of ends of two tomatoes Dice up the two tomatoes Add to the same medium mixing bowl Remove stems from cilantro Chop 1/4 cup cilantro Add to the same medium mixing bowl Cut lime in half Squeeze lime over mixing bowl ingredients Sprinkle salt into mixing bowl Mix ingredients in the medium mixing bowl Add medium mixing bowl ingredients into large mixing bowl with avocado Stir all ingredients until evenly mixed Add more ingredients for desired taste
